I am Jillyan - Blessed to be sharing my Fabric Art with you! I find peace in creating truly unique items using yarn and a small hook. This craft is called Crochet. The Crochet Stitch is one that can not be replicated using a machine, setting it apart from knit items. Each individual stitch is made using my own fingers, set with positive intention, & created with love.

LoopyDarlin is a one woman shop, offering custom ordering for clothing, home decor, & other accessories. Based in a rapidly expanding mountain town, I have dreams to live a country life, away from the noise & fast pace of the city. Sustainable living and solar energy is very important to me, and I wish to incorporate it into my life. Your support helps me to fund these dreams of living off grid.

After an order is placed, I will go on the search for the yarn best suited for the project & for your budget. Each and every stitch will be made with you in mind. When creating, I enjoy using "natural fiber" yarns such as Merino Wool, Baby Alpaca, & Bamboo Cotton. As best I can, I source my yarn locally or I order online from one of my favourite small shops. I believe that Beauty Takes Time - I am never in a hurry to finish a creation! That being said, I am happy to work on a deadline for you! Let's create something Loopy together!
